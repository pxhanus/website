Prelude

I am a homeschooled high school student from Nebraska. My interests lie in Computer Science, Psychology, and Music. I mentor for Academy Robotics & Science Club. I study autism with Kami Koldewyn in the Kanwisher Lab in Massachusetts Institute of Technology's Brain and Cognitive Science Department. I have studied classical voice under Matthew Harden at the University of Nebraska at Omaha for six years.

My goal is to combine my interests in Computer Science, Psychology, and Music first, at a top undergraduate institution, and then as a successful member of society.
